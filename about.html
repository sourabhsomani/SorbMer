About Sörb & Mer

Our Story

Two Decades of Excellence

Founded in 1999, Sörb & Mer began as a small workshop in Milan with a singular vision: to create furniture that would stand the test of time in the world's most prestigious hospitality spaces.

What started as a passion for craftsmanship has evolved into a global operation, yet our core values remain unchanged. We believe that exceptional furniture is born from the marriage of traditional techniques and modern innovation.

Today, we partner with leading architects, interior designers, and developers across 50 countries, delivering furniture solutions that transform spaces and exceed expectations.

Our Approach

How We Work

01

Discovery & Consultation

We begin by understanding your vision, requirements, and constraints. Our team visits the site, reviews architectural plans, and discusses your design intent in detail.

02

Design Development

Our designers create detailed drawings and 3D renderings, refining every aspect until the design perfectly aligns with your vision. We provide material samples and finishes for approval.

03

Prototype & Sampling

Before full production, we create prototypes for critical pieces, allowing you to experience the quality, comfort, and aesthetics firsthand.

04

Manufacturing

Our state-of-the-art facility brings designs to life with precision craftsmanship. Quality checks at every stage ensure consistency across all pieces.

05

Delivery & Installation

We coordinate logistics worldwide and provide professional installation services, ensuring every piece is perfectly placed and finished.
