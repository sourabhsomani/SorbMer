FAQ

Common Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A typical hotel project takes 12-18 months from concept to completion. We provide detailed timelines during the proposal phase.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely. We have completed projects in over 50 countries and have extensive experience with international logistics, customs, and installation coordination.

Can you work with our existing designs?

Yes, we can manufacture from your existing designs or collaborate with your design team to refine concepts for production. We also offer full design services if needed.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We work on projects of all sizes. While we specialize in large-scale hospitality projects, we're happy to discuss smaller projects and provide guidance on the best approach.
