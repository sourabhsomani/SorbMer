Resort Project

The Azure Resort

Maldives | 2023 | 200 Rooms
Overview

A Paradise of Design

The Azure Resort represents the pinnacle of beachfront luxury. Located on a private island in the Maldives, this 200-room property required furniture that would complement its stunning natural surroundings while providing the ultimate in guest comfort.

Our team worked closely with the resort's design team to create custom pieces that blend contemporary aesthetics with local materials and craftsmanship. The result is a cohesive design language that flows throughout the property—from guest villas to restaurants to the spa.

Every piece was engineered to withstand the harsh marine environment while maintaining its beauty and functionality for years to come.
